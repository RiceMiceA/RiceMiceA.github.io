Engineering journey

The common thread is making systems real.

My projects have moved from algorithms and course-scale demos toward integrated systems: autonomous planning, robot arms, PCB/firmware development, mixed reality interfaces, BLE bridges, and AI-backed physical computing.

The direction I want this site to capture is not just "here are repos," but "here is how I think through engineering tradeoffs." As the projects mature, each post can become a build log with photos, schematics, demo videos, failures, and the final result.
