About

Robotics is where mechanical constraints, electrons, code, and human intent meet.

My work sits at the intersection of robot motion, embedded systems, and hands-on engineering. I like projects where a clean algorithm has to survive a messy bench: hardware resets, network configuration, camera calibration, ROS launch files, and the final demo pressure test.

This portfolio is designed to feel like a build archive rather than a generic resume. Each project entry highlights the engineering question, the tools used, and the next thing I want to improve.
